### Caching Strategy
- `GET /api/artifacts` → `public, max-age=60, stale-while-revalidate=120`
- `GET /api/artifacts/:id` → `public, max-age=300, stale-while-revalidate=600`
- `GET /health` → `public, max-age=30, stale-while-revalidate=60`
- All other routes → `no-store, no-cache, must-revalidate`
